Serves: 4

Prep: 20 Mins

Cook: 5 Hours 30 Mins

Bolognese Bake SC

Beef
Slow Cook
Bake
Pasta

Ingredients

  • 400g Minced Beef
  • 500g Passata
  • 1 tin Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 1 Medium Carrot, diced
  • 1 stick Celery, diced
  • 5 Mushrooms, diced
  • 1 Pepper, diced
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, crushed
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 Beef Stock Cubes
  • ½ tsp oregano
  • ½ tsp basil
  • ½ tsp rosemary
  • 200g Pasta
  • 250ml Boiling Water

Instructions

  1. Season mince with salt and pepper and set aside. Put everything except pasta and water into a slow cooker. Cook on high for 5 hours.
  2. Add boiling water and pasta and stir well. Cook for another 25-30 minutes
  3. Season and serve.
