Serves: 4-6

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 6-8 Hours

Chicken, Bean, and Chorizo One Pot

Chicken
Slow Cook
Easy
Chorizo

Ingredients

  • 1 Red Onion, diced
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, chopped
  • 1 tin Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 tins Cannellini Beans, drained
  • 1 large Sweet Potato, diced
  • 2-3 Chorizo Sausages, skinned
  • 4-6 Chicken Thighs
  • 250ml Chicken Stock
  • 1 tsp Tomato puree
  • Pinch Cayenne Pepper
  • Pinch Smoked Paprika
  • 2 heaped tsp Cornflour, mixed with a little water
  • Crunchy bread and salad to serve

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in the slow cooker and combine well. Season to taste and add more stock if needed.
  2. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
  3. Serve with crusty bread and salad.
