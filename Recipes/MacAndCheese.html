Serves: 4

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 60 Mins

Mac & Cheese

Pasta
Bake
Midweek
Quick

Ingredients

  • 500g Macaroni
  • 300ml Full Fat Milk
  • 1 pack Bacon
  • 50g Frozen Sweetcorn
  • Salt & Pepper to season
  • 1 Cheese Sauce Packet
  • Salad, to serve (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 fan. Boil a pan of water and cook macaroni to the time listed on the package (approximately 10 mins).
  2. Whilst pasta is cooking, chop bacon into small, bitesize pieces and fry until cooked.
  3. Mix up cheese sauce packet and combine bacon, pasta and cheese sauce into one baking dish. Cook in the oven for 30 minutes. Serve with sweetcorn and/or side salad.
