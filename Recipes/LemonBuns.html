Serves: 12

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 22 Mins

Lemon Buns

Desserts
Buns
Citrus

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • Caster sugar, same weight as eggs
  • Butter, same weight as eggs
  • Self-raising flour, same weight as eggs
  • 1 Lemon, juice and zest
  • 1 bag Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees. Beat butter and sugar together. Add eggs one at a time and beat into mixture. Mix in lemon zest. Mix in flour gently and mix with a mixer. Pour in chocolate chips and fold.
  2. Share out mixture between muffin cases using a couple of dessert spoons. Mixture should just about drop off of the spoon.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven for 22 minutes. DO NOT OPEN WHILST COOKING. Should spring back when pushed gently on their tops when cooked but check quickly so as not to let too much eat out.
