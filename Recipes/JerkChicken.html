Serves: 4

Prep: 20 Mins

Cook: 60 Mins

Jerk Chicken

Traybake
Chicken

Ingredients

  • 6 Skin on, bone in, Chicken thighs
  • 90g Jerk Paste
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 Pineapple, Peeled, Cored and Chopped into Chunks (or 1 tin of pineapple)
  • 2 Sweet Potatoes Peeled and Chopped into Chunks
  • 1 Sliced Red Pepper
  • 300g Wholegrain Rice
  • 1 tin Black Beans
  • 1 tbsp Coconut Oil

Instructions

  1. Heat to 160 fan (180C/gas 4). Cut thighs in half down one side of the bone. Put in a large roasting tin and cover with sauce (jerk past mixed with lime zest and juice). Tuck pineapple pieces, sweet potatoes and peppers in around the chicken. Season and cover. Roast for 30 minutes
  2. Increase temperature to 200 fan (220C/gas 7), remove foil and baste the chicken. Stir and return to over for 20 - 25 minutes
  3. Cook rice and stir in beans, coconut oil, thyme leaves and seasoning. Cook for 2-3 minutes to warm beans through.
  4. Serve 3 pieces of chicken per person with vegetables, lettuce and rice on the side.
