Serves: 2

Prep: 20 Mins

Cook: 45 Mins

Schnitzel and Scalloped Potatoes

Chicken
Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Breast (per person)
  • 1 Egg, Beaten
  • Breadcrumbs, as needed
  • 3 Potatoes, sliced thinly
  • Oil
  • Parmesan
  • Salt & Pepper, to taste
  • Veg/Salad to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and season breadcrumbs with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Put chicken breast in a bag and flatten with a rolling pin. Coat in egg and then breadcrumbs.Fry for 3 minutes on each side. Put in oven (preheated at 180) for 10 minutes.
  3. Arrange potatoes in a single layer in an oven-proof dish.Season and cover with a splash of oil. Cook for 45 minutes, checking regularly to make sure it doesnâ€™t burn.
  4. Serve with vegetables or a side salad.
