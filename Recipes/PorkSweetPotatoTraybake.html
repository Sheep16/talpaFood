Serves: 4

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 20 Mins

Pork and Sweet Potato Bake

Pork
Traybake
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 4 thick Pork Chops
  • 4 Sweet Potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
  • 4 Garlic CLoves, unpeeled
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 1 Lime, juice and zest
  • 1 Red Chilli, deseeded and sliced
  • 2 Spring Onions, sliced
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Place oil, ground coriander and seasoning into a bowl and mix. Toss pork chops, sweet potatoes, and garlic in oil and spread out in a shallow baking tray. Cook in oven for 10 minutes.
  2. Whilst cooking, stir honey, lime zest, and lime juice. Turn meat over and drizzle honey mix over. Return to oven for a further 10 minutes until golden and cooked through.
  3. Serve with chilli and spring onions as toppings.
