Beefy Noods

Ingredients

  • 1 Spring Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 2cm Fresh Ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 300g Green Beans, cut into 2cm batons
  • 125g Beef Mince
  • 2 portions of Egg Noodles

Instructions

  1. Make sauce in a large bowl by adding spring onion, a quarter of the garlic and ginger, the soy sauce, honey, and Â½ tbsp of olive oil. Beat together until honey has dissolved.
  2. Add green beans to a frying pan with remaining oil, garlic, ginger, and beef mince. Meanwhile, cook noodles in a pan of boiling water.
  3. When cooked, drain and toss in the sauce. Pour noodles and sauce into the frying pan and combine. Serve immediately.
