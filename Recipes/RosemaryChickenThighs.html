Serves: 4

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 45 Mins

Rosemary Roast Chicken Thighs

Chicken
Tray Bake
Easy

Ingredients

  • 8 Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Garlic Bulb, cloves separated
  • 1 Lemon, halved
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 pack Green Beans
  • Dried Rosemary to taste
  • 750g New Potatoes

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 180. Put potatoes, green beans, garlic cloves, oil, salt, and pepper in a large roasting dish. Squeeze all juice from both lemon halves, then cut them into chunks and add to the dish. Toss everything together, cover dish with foil and roast for about 15 mins
  2. Remove foil, add rosemary and place seasoned chicken thighs on top. Roast for another 30-35 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked and potatoes are tender.
  3. Serve with extra vegetables.
