Chipotle Spiced Chicken

Bake
s

Ingredients

  • 450g Potatoes
  • Mexican Spices
  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 tbsp Chipotle Paste
  • 1 Green Pepper, cut into thin slices
  • 1 Garlic Clove, grated
  • 1 Red Onion, thinly sliced
  • 75g Sour Cream

Instructions

  1. Chop potatoes, sprinkle with oil and Mexican spice. Toss together and roast for 25-30 minutes at 180 degrees. Turn halfway through.
  2. Marinate chicken in Â½ chipotle spice and oil. Prep the rest of the veg and mix remaining chipotle paste and sour cream.
  3. Cook chicken for 4-5 minutes on each side in a hot frying pan. Transfer to a baking tray and roast until cooked through, approximately 12-15 minutes. Pan fry the vegetables for approximately 6-8 minutes and season to taste.
  4. When chicken is cooked, leave to rest for a couple minutes. Add vegetables to the potato tray and mix together. Cut each chicken breast into 5 slices and serve on a bed of potatoes and veg. Finish off with sour cream chipotle mix on top.
