Serves: 4

Prep: 30 Mins

Cook: 2 Hours

Roast Chicken

Chicken
Roast

Ingredients

  • Large Whole Chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • 3 Large Potatoes
  • Seasonal Vegetables
  • Yorkshire Puddings
  • Butter and Flour

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 180. Place whole chicken in a metal roasting tray. Pour oil over the chicken. Add a sprinkle of paprika, rosemary, salt, and pepper to the chicken and rub into the skin. Cover with foil and roast for 90 minutes.
  2. Peel and chop the potatoes. Boil for 20 minutes (for roast potatoes) or 30 minutes (for mash). If roast potatoes, preheat some oil in another roasting tray in the oven. Cook for 40 minutes, turning occasionally.
  3. Remove chicken from oven. Check if it’s cooked by twisting a leg. If the bone pops out then it’s cooked. Drain off the juices and put the chicken back in (without foil) to crisp up for 30 minutes.
  4. Cook seasonal veg depending on what vegetables are being used. After chicken has cooked, remove and allow to rest whilst making gravy. Put yorkshire puddings in the oven for 5 minutes.
  5. Gravy: Melt 1 tbsp of butter in a pan and add 1 tbsp of flour to create a roux. Use more if serving a larger group of people. Slowly pour in the chicken juices, making sure to stir continuously. Add some lemon juice and mix.
  6. Serve when ready.
