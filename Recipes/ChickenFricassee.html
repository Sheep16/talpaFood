Serves: 2-4

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 15 Mins

Chicken Fricassee

Chicken
Rice
Easy

Ingredients

  • 150g Rice
  • 75g Frozen Peas
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 400g Chicken Breast, stripped
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 1 Garlic Clove, minced
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 150ml White Wine
  • 150ml Chicken Stock
  • 150ml Double Cream
  • Sprinkle of Parsley
  • Pinch of Nutmeg
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions. Cook peas for 2-3 minutes in boiling water and set aside.
  2. Heat butter in a frying pan. Add chicken and cook until browned. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. Add onion and cook for 2-3 minutes until softened. Add garlic and thyme and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Add chicken back into pan.
  4. Turn up heat and add wine. Reduce and add stock. Cook for 5 minutes until thickened. Remove from heat and add remaining ingredients, including peas.
  5. Serve with rice.
