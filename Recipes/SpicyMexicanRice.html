Serves: 6

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 30 Mins

Spicy Mexican Rice

Vegetarian
Rice
Vegan
Quick

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 2 crushed Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Green Chilli, sliced
  • 1 Green Pepper, diced
  • 200g Basmati Rice
  • 1 tbsp Chipotle Paste
  • 1 tin Chopped Tomatoes
  • 250ml Vegetable Stock

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a deep, lidded frying pan. Cook onion, garlic, chilli and pepper for 10 minutes until starting to soften. Stir in rice and then cook for 3-4 minutes whilst continually stirring.
  2. Mix chipotle paste and cook, stirring for a couple of minutes until fragrant. Pour the tomatoes and stock in and stir well.
  3. Bring to a dimmer then turn heat to low, put the lid on and cook for 15 minutes. Stir through coriander and serve with lime wedges.
