Chicken and Chorizo Paella

Chicken
Rice
Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 200g Chorizo, sliced
  • 400g Chicken, diced
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 300g Paella Rice
  • 1l Chicken Stock
  • 150g Frozen Peas
  • Splash Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Fry onion for 2-3 minutes until softened and add garlic for 1 minute. Add chorizo and chicken and brown for a couple of minutes.
  2. Add paprika and rice before adding the stock. Cook for 12-15 minutes, until rice has absorbed most of the liquid. Add peas after 5 minutes.
  3. Stir through seasoning and add lemon juice before serving.
