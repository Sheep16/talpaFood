Serves: 5

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 30 Mins

Tuscan Butter Chicken

Chicken
Curry
Bake
Quick

Ingredients

  • 600g Chicken Breast, diced
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 30g Butter
  • Handful of Spinach
  • 200ml Milk
  • 60g Cream Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • Serve with Rice

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with the oregano, oil, garlic powder, cumin, and chilli flakes. Pan fry on high heat for 10-15 minutes, until chicken is cooked.
  2. Turn heat down and add the onion, butter, tomato paste, milk, spinach, and cream cheese. Leave to simmer until creamy.
  3. Serve with rice. Leftovers can last up to 3 days in the fridge or 3 months in the freezer.
