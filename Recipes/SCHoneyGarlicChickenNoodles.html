Serves: 4

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 4-8 Hours

SC Honey Garlic Chicken Noodles

Chicken
Slow Cook
Noodles
Takeaway

Ingredients

  • 400g Chicken Thighs
  • 7 tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
  • 7 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Cornflour (mixed with 1 tbsp water)
  • 1 Garlic bulb
  • 4 tbsp Sriracha
  • 100ml Chicken Stock
  • 200g Noodles
  • 1 tbsp Hoisin Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Ginger

Instructions

  1. Add everything except noodles to the slow cooker.Cook for 4-5 hours on high or 7-8 hours on low.
  2. Add in cooked noodles and serve.
  3. Optional: can cook the noodles in the SC by adding 300ml of chicken stock and cooking for 15-20 minutes.
