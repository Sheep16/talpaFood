Serves: 6

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 60 Mins

Spanish Chicken Traybake

Chicken
Spanish
Tray Bake
Easy

Ingredients

  • 12 Chicken Thighs, bone-in
  • 750g Chorizo
  • 1kg New Potatoes, halved
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Red Onions, chopped
  • 1 Orange Zest
  • 2 tbsp Oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 fan. Put oil in the bottom of 2 shallow roasting tins, 1 tbsp in each. Rub the skin of the chicken in the oil and then turn skin-side up. 6 thighs per tray.
  2. Divide chorizo and new potatoes between the 2 tins evenly. Sprinkle the onion, oregano, and orange zest over the tins.
  3. Cook for 1 hour, swapping the top tray with the bottom and basting half-way through.
