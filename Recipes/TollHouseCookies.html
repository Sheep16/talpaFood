Serves: Many

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 12 Mins

Toll House Cookies

Desserts
Cookies

Ingredients

  • 200g Butter
  • 175g Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • ½ tsp Bircarbonate of Soda
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 1 bag Chocolate Chips
  • 150g Plain Flour

Instructions

  1. Cream marg and sugar, add egg beaten whole and mix well. Add Flour, salt and soda mixed together. Lastly add chocolate chips (or ½ cup of nuts or cherries or anything else you like!)
  2. Drop by 1/2 tsp onto greased bakingtray (they spread a lot so don't put them too close together). Bake 10 to 12 minutes in 160 degrees. Put them straight on a cooling rack , don't leave them on the tray or you will have to chip them off.
  3. This mixture makes about 50 to 60. Will freeze, with freezer wrap between layers, but not so crisp afterwards obviously. Have been known to eat them frozen !
