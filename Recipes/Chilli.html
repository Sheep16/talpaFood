Serves: 6

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 30 Mins

Chilli Con Carne

Beef
Easy
Rice

Ingredients

  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 2 tins Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 Green Pepper, diced
  • 1 Red Pepper, diced
  • 2 tins Kidney Beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 Beef Oxo Cubes
  • Chilli Powder
  • Cayenne Pepper

Instructions

  1. Brown mince and drain off any excess liquid. Add onions, fry for a couple minutes and add peppers.
  2. Add seasoning, stir together, and add tomatoes. Add oxo cubes and kidney beans and stir.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer until liquid is reduced. Serve with rice, jacket potato, wraps, tortilla chips.
