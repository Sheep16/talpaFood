Serves: 2

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 50 Mins

Lemon Chicken

Chicken
Rice
Midweek
Quick

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts, whole
  • 175g White Rice
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • 2 tsp Lemon Juice
  • 75g Peas
  • Salt & Pepper to Season
  • 1 Chicken Stock cube

Instructions

  1. Boil kettle and make enough chicken stock to come halfway up the chicken breasts.
  2. Put chicken breasts in a casserole dish and pour in chicken stock and lemon juice. Season and cover.
  3. Cook for 40 minutes and then separate sauce from chicken. Place back in the oven to keep warm.
  4. Melt butter in a small pan and add flour to make a roux. Slowly pour in chicken juices, mixing to create a gravy.
  5. Serve with boiled rice and peas. Also works with mashed potato
