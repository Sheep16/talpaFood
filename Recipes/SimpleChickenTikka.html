Simple Chicken Tikka

Chicken
Tray Bake
Easy
Oven

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts, chopped into bite size chunks
  • 2 tbsp yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp tikka curry paste

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 200. Mix yoghurt and tikka curry. Mix with the chicken. Marinade between 2 and 12 hours.
  2. Spread onto a baking tray in a single layer. Cook for 10-15 minutes, turning half way through.
  3. Serve with rice, in a wrap for lunches, etc.
