Serves: 2

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 45 Mins

Sausage and Potato Traybake

Pork
Traybake
Easy

Ingredients

  • 500g Baby Potatoes
  • 3 Sausages
  • 2 Red Onions, cut into 8 segments each
  • 1 Pepper, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced

Instructions

  1. Boil kettle and preheat oven to 180. Cut any particularly large potatoes in half and add potatoes to a pan of boiling water. Cook for 10 minutes.
  2. Cut sausages into three pieces each. Add sausage, onion, and pepper to a large oven tray. Pour over oil, season and shake to coat.
  3. Drain potatoes and cover in butter and garlic. Put in oven tray and roast for 20 minutes. Serve when cooked.
