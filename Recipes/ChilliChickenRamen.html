Serves: 2

Chilli Chicken Ramen

Chicken
Ramen
Takeaway

Ingredients

  • 500ml chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp of sriracha sauce
  • 156g of good egg noodles
  • 1 chicken breast, marinated
  • Handful of bean sprouts
  • Â¼ red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 spring onion, chopped
  • 1 fresh red chilli, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp of freshly grated ginger

Instructions

  1. Mix marinate by combining soy sauce and ginger into a bowl. Marinate chicken for 1 hour before placing in a preheated oven until cooked through (preheated 180 degrees, cook for 40 minutes).
  2. Cook egg noodles according to package instructions. Drain and pour into a ramen bowl. Add sriracha sauce and chicken stock. Stir to separate noodles. Slice chicken and place on top of the noodles.
  3. Add freshly chopped remaining ingredients to dish and serve.
