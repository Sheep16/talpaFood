Serves: 4

Prep: 20 Mins

Cook: 30 Mins

Cottage Pie

Beef
Bake

Ingredients

  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 3 Carrots, chopped
  • 3 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  • 2 Beef OXO Cubes
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp Cornflour
  • Potatoes, cooked and mashed with butter

Instructions

  1. Brown mince beef and drain off excess fat. Add onions and fry for a couple of minutes. Add carrots, tomato ketchup, oxo cubes, and a splash of boiling water. Mix to combine. Add worcestershire sauce and cornflour (mixed with water)
  2. Pour into a glass dish and top with mashed potato. Cook in oven for 30 minutes at 180 degrees.
  3. Serve with green veg, corn, and/or garlic bread
