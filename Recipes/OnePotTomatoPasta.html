Serves: 2

One-Pot Pasta in Tomato Ragu

Pasta
Vegetarian
East
One pot

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Stock Cube
  • 3 tsp Mixed Herbs
  • Â¼ tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tin Chopped Tomatoes
  • 150g Penne Pasta
  • 1 Onion, finely sliced
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 4-6 Sundried Tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 50g Feta, crumbled

Instructions

  1. Add 300ml boiling water to a deep saucepan and crumble in chicken stock. Add mixed herbs, chilli flakes, and chopped tomatoes. Add pasta, onion, garlic, and sundried tomatoes and stir.
  2. Cover and cook for 10 minutes. Remove lid and continue to boil for another 7 minutes.
  3. Stir feta through, taste and season if needed. Serve immediately.
