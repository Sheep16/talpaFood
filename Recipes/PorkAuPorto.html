Serves: 4

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 45 Mins

Pork Au Porto

Pork
Fancy
Rice
Casserole

Ingredients

  • 750g Pork Fillet
  • 25g Flour
  • 25g Butter
  • 2 Onions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Mushroom Ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Redcurrant Jelly
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 4 tbsp Red Wine/Port
  • 4 tbsp Double Cream

Instructions

  1. Remove outer skin from pork fillet and cut into 1 inch cubes. Coat with seasoned flour and saute in melted butter until golden. Transfer to a casserole dish.
  2. Saute onions in remaining butter (add more if needed). Add to casserole dish. Mix together all other ingredients (except cream) and pour over meat.
  3. Cook in oven for 30-40 minutes at 180 degrees. Spoon cream over meat, stir and return to oven for 5 minutes to heat through. Serve with rice and peas.
