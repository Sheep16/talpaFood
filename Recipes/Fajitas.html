Cook: 30 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 1 Red Pepper, sliced
  • 1 Yellow Pepper, sliced
  • 2 Chicken Breasts, sliced
  • approx. 2 tbsp Nando’s Medium Sauce
  • Wraps
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Put sliced chicken and onion into a bowl. Pour desired amount of Nando’s Peri Peri sauce and mix. (Optional: Marinade for up to 12 hours.)
  2. Pour chicken and onion into a hot wok and cook for 5 minutes, until chicken is browned.
  3. Add pepper and cook until chicken is fully cooked. Serve with wraps and grated cheese.
