Serves: 3

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 20 Mins

Bacon Mozzerella Pasta

Pork
Pasta
Quick

Ingredients

  • 400g pasta shells
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 8 rashers of bacon
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 142ml double cream
  • 2 balls mozzerella

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over a medium heat, add the onion and cook until soft and golden.
  2. Add the bacon and fry until golden.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, bring to a simmer and cook gently over a low heat for 5 minutes.
  5. Add cream and stir until heated through. Rip the mozzerella into small pieces and heat until melted.
  6. Drain pasta and add sauce. Mix until combined and serve.
