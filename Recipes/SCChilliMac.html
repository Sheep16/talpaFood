Serves: 6-8

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 4 Hours

SC Chilli Mac

Beef
Slow Cooker
Bake
Pasta

Ingredients

  • 500g Extra Lean Ground Beef or Lean Ground Turkey
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, pressed or finely minced
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 2 tbsp Chili Powder (mild)
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • ¼ tsp table salt
  • ½ tsp Pepper
  • ¼ tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 3 Green chilli, deseeded and chopped
  • 1 tin Diced Tomatoes
  • 700ml Beef Stock
  • 2 tins Beans (red kidney, black, or pinto) drained & rinsed
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 250g Small Elbow Macaroni

Instructions

  1. Add the meat, onion, garlic, bay leaf, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, oregano, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper. Stir. If your ground beef isn't lean, just brown it and drain fat before adding.
  2. Add the green chiles, diced tomatoes, beef stock, beans, and tomato paste. Stir well. Cook on Low for 6-8 hours, or High for 3-4 hours.
  3. If you add the pasta dry, add it 20 minutes before serving and cook on Low. If adding cooked pasta, add just before serving.
  4. Taste and adjust salt, if needed. Garnish with your choice of sour cream, cheese, avocado, diced onion, corn chips, cilantro, etc.
