Serves: 4

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 30 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 3 tbsp Flour
  • 1 Lime
  • 500ml Milk
  • 1 tsp Ground Nutmeg
  • 350g Macaroni
  • 300g Wholegrain Rice
  • 185g Runner Beans
  • 220g Green Beans
  • 140g Mature Cheddar (half grated, half chopped)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan over a medium heat and add the flour. Stir well and, once combined, keep stirring whilst pouring a little milk in. Mix until smooth. Repeat until all milk has been added. Stir in nutmeg and seasoning and then remove from heat.
  2. Heat oven to 200 fan (220C / gas 7). Boil a large pan of water and add macaroni. Boil for 8 minutes then add the beans. Boil for another 3 minutes.
  3. Drain pasta and beans and pour into a large baking dish. Pour over the cause and tuck chopped cheese in among the pasta. Bake for 10-15 minutes. Serve with salad.
