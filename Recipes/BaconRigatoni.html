Serves: 2

Prep: 5 Mins

Cook: 30 Mins

Bacon Rigatoni

Pork
Pasta
One Day
Quick

Ingredients

  • 1 Aubergine
  • 1 Garlic Clove, pressed
  • 1 Medium Tomato, chopped
  • 100g Feta Cheese, crumbled
  • 200g Rigatoni Pasta
  • 90g Bacon Lardons
  • 400g Tinned Tomatoes
  • Pinch of Sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 fan. Boil large saucepan of water for the pasta. Trim the aubergine, half it lengthways and then cut into roughly 1cm x 1cm chunks.
  2. Put the aubergine on a tray and season with salt, pepper, and oil. Roast for 18-25 minutes, until soft and browned. Turn half way through. Meanwhile, cook the rigatoni for about 12 minutes and toss with oil to stop it sticking.
  3. Heat oil in pan and cook the bacon lardons for 5-6 minutes, until browned. Add garlic and chilli flakes. Add chopped tomatoes, salt and a pinch of sugar. Lower the heat and simmer until it has reduced by half. Add in the aubergine.
  4. Grill the feta and fresh tomato in the same pan that was used for the aubergine. Combine and serve immediately.
