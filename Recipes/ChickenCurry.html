Serves: 6

Prep: 10 Mins

Cook: 30 Mins

Chicken Curry

Chicken
Rice
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts, diced
  • Onions, diced
  • 2 Peppers, diced
  • 1 Apple, peeled, cored, and diced
  • Handful of raisins
  • 2 Chicken Oxo Cubes
  • 1 tbsp Patak’s Curry Paste
  • ¼ block Coconut

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large frying pan and seal chicken. Add onions and fry for a couple of minutes. Then add pepper and apple. Add in curry paste and stir to coat.
  2. Crumble in oxo cubes and add half a pint of boiling water. Add raisins and coconut. Leave to simmer.
  3. Serve with rice, chips etc.
